Cappuccino

Cappuccino is an Italian coffee beverage that is formed by combining dual espresso shots with steam milk. It is one of the most famous types of coffee.

Robusta

Robusta beans are the second most popular coffee bean in the world, and they are known for their strong, bold flavour and high caffeine content.

Arabica

Arabica beans are the most widely cultivated and consumed coffee beans in the world. These beans are grown at high altitudes and in cooler climates.

Irish

Irish coffee is a variety of hot coffee that involves bourbon, hot coupled shots of espresso and sugar, covered with dense milk lather.

Liberica

Liberica beans are a rare type of coffee bean that is grown primarily in Southeast Asia. These beans have a unique flavour profile.

Bella Kaapi

Coorg’s authentic Bella Kaapi is a personal favourite. This coffee is perfect for those who enjoy black coffee.
